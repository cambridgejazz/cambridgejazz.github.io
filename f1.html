Jazz is for ALL

We are a non profit organization and our aim is to **organise and promote a jazz festival of international, national and local artists in collaboration with the universities and venues in Cambridge, in order to bring a diverse spectrum of jazz through different forms of media, to a wider audience of adults, students and schoolchildren and to promote jazz education.

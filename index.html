Cambridge Jazz Festival 25-29 November 2015

Cambridge's first jazz festival is coming in 2015!

Featuring international, national and local acts over 5 exciting days of concerts taking place across the universities, schools and the city.

With workshops, education programmes and school performances.

The fabulous pianist, composer and broadcaster Julian Joseph plays on Saturday 28th.

Clare Cellars, Clare College

       Trinity Lane, Cambridge, CB2 1TL
       Capacity 192
       Clare Cellars is located in the crypts underneath Clare chapel
       and is one of the most intimate and distinctive venues in Cambridge.
       The main area is based under the famous brick arches with a comprehensive bar and shooters bar.
